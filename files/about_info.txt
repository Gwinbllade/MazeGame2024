    Hey there! Welcome to Wonder Maze
Labyrinths are a fascinating type of puzzle, and they present a different type of challenge to the brain.
Neuropsychobiological studies show that solving mazes activates a network in the brain
from the visual to the parietal areas.
So it is not only interesting and fun, but also useful.

How to play?
The “player” is controlled by the keys up, left, right, down.





Have fun and reach the top, player!
